Our Mission

LDM Academy believes in the holistic approach to child development. Our curriculum is designed to focus on the creative, emotional, intellectual, physical, and social growth of each individual. You will find our environment clean, safe and health oriented. The purpose of our approach is to foster competency at the earliest possible age. While the emphasis is on the children, family involvement is encouraged and supported.

We have several goals for children:

To be competent and confident in their abilities; to be self-directed in a constructive, creative manner

To be successful in future educational pursuits

To develop a love for learning and the ability to know how to learn

To develop self-control and a sense of right and wrong

To feel good about who they are

To learn cooperation with other children as well as adults

To reach their full potential in emotional, intellectual, physical and social development

Ages - Six (6) weeks to four (4) years

The Voluntarily Pre Kindergarten Program is offered free to Florida residents who have children that will be four years of age by September 1st of every school year. See a member of administration for details.

Hours of Operation: Monday through Friday, 7:00 a.m. - 6:00 p.m.
