About Us

Our Mission

Our mission statement is "To win the lost at any cost and encourage the redeemed by Godly means, while building people of faith, power and praise."

Our Vision

The mission will be met through evangelism, media outreach and other forms of care to help each person come into a saving knowledge of Jesus Christ and come to understand how to maximize their God-given potential in a manner which glorifies God.

Further, we will build people of faith, power and praise through teaching God's word, providing wholesome fellowship where God is glorified, and through authentic worship that exceeds all expectations. We commit to meeting spiritual needs and providing examples of uncompromising integrity and excellence.

Finally, through a focused commitment to the next generation, we will develop their leadership potential so that the youth will take their place as believers and understand their God-assigned destiny and purpose.
